Labyrinth Descension
09.08.2025
Synopsis
   In the Labyrinth Descension, decend into a mad world of mosters and infected humans with your party of 4, to find out what is killing your world. A mysterious infection spread across earth, steaming from this facility. Decent into the facility and document what you find, be careful to watch out for the inhabitants.

Explore this dark landscape with just a lantern, and try to survive.

This JRPG rougelike follows a party of 4 into a dark dungeon where they must survive. In this game we take the best aspects of 2 genres. With a story driven game loop, and turn based combat, aswell as randomly generated dungeon levels and perma-death, If you enjoy a challenge this is your game. Staying with the theme of mixing and matching themes, this game will feature an HD-2D perspective. 2D assets existing in a 3d world.
