Fae Forest
09.08.2025
Synopsis
   In The Fae Forest, you fall into the fairy world and fight to get your lost sister back. Traveling through the forest, fighting the fae to get your sister back. Decypher the mysteries of this world and use them to your advantage.

Explore this dark landscape with just a lantern, and try to survive.

This stealth game is set in a fanciful environment, much different from your mainstream stealth games. For stealth players looking for a change in scenery, Fae Forest is a perfect fit. Find and evade mythical creatures such as fae, goblins, gnomes, gargoyls, and many more. Utilize your knowledge from the human world to outsmart the overlords of the world.
