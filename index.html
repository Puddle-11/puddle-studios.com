PUDDLE STUDIO
ABOUT
  Puddle Studios is a solo indie dev studio founded by Puddle (Rowan Byington), Puddle started developing games in 2020, and specializes in digital art and programming, having developed multiple games fully solo, With his most recent project "Forgotten God" planned to be his first commercial release.

At 12, he started with C++ and continued until 14, when he tried Unreal Engine but switched to C# and Unity after facing challenges. After a month learning Unity, Puddle began releasing small games, gradually progressing to demo-like projects. Alongside programming, Puddle pursued art, creating posters for his local school and sharing work on social media. As his skills grew, game development became more than a hobby. Discovering Full Sail University, Puddle applied for their Emerging Tech Scholarship.
Though initially unsuccessful with his "Labyrinth" submission, he reapplied with "Planetarium" and earned the scholarship. Now in college, Puddle works on personal and school projects, including "MechMaze," while further developing his skills.
